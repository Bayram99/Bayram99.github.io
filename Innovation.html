IBIN / SMART BIN

The aim of our product is to show how viable our concept is and that smart bins can really be the future. This is especially important looking at the current market, cuts to the waste management and current bin strikes. The system can also be implemented to smaller office bins where especially in a big office complex can save time and money due to the cleaners only going to bins that are full. This system can easily be implemented into many types of bins and is universal.
The following idea was award winning project in Birmingham City University Innovation Fest 2019.
