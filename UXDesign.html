UX Design

The application’s (Sports One) aim is to provide users with information about upcoming sporting events. Whether the user is looking for restaurants, bars, accommodation, or location of the upcoming event they are all provided in the same app, the combination of these features distinguishes the app from its competition.

This UX Design was first implemented on a paper prototype (Slide 1) then low fidelity digital wireframe and eventually High fidelity prototype, it addresses different accessibility issues with solutions, Dark mode for users with low vision (Slide 2), good size and easy to reach buttons for users with physical impairment, haptic feature which vibrates the phone for when the selected game starts to accommodate users with hearing impairment and the auto complete & automatic suggestions for people with cognitive impairment.
This Responsive Design (Slide 3) was the result of testing conducted by 12 participants (ages range between 20 – 35 and they all are familiar with using mobile applications.) using 5 different data collection techniques(Think Aloud, Interview, SUS questionnaire, Success rate and Error rate).
